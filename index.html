Services

What's included.

Professional websites, done properly. I take care of everything — from the first message to the site going live.

Design & Layout

Every site is designed from scratch to match your business. No generic templates — a clean, modern look built around who you are.

  • Custom design built around your brand
  • Professional typography and colours
  • Clear structure that guides visitors
  • Consistent, polished look throughout
Mobile & Speed

The site will look and work perfectly on phones, tablets and desktops. Mobile is always the priority — most visitors are on their phone.

  • Fully responsive on every screen size
  • Fast loading on mobile connections
  • Touch-friendly buttons and navigation
  • Clean, lightweight code
Copy & Content

I write all the text for the page. Clear, direct copy that tells visitors who you are, what you offer and how to get in touch.

  • Headline and intro that explains the business
  • Description of your services or offer
  • Short about section
  • Contact section with your details
Domain & Publishing

I publish the finished site to a real web address. If you have a domain I work with it. If not, I guide you through getting one — it takes minutes.

  • Guidance on buying a domain if needed
  • Site published to your web address
  • SSL certificate included (https)
  • Free hosting for static sites
SEO-Ready Copy

The page is written so Google can find and read it properly. No complicated setup — just clean structure and the right words from the start.

  • SEO-friendly page title and headline
  • Meta description for search results
  • Alt text on all images
  • Clean, crawlable page structure
Launch & 30-Day Support

Once the site is approved, I publish it and stay available for 30 days. Two rounds of revisions are included — free of charge.

  • Full review and testing before going live
  • Tested on mobile and desktop
  • 2 rounds of revisions included free
  • 30 days of support via message
